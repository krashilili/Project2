Wind Speed Analysis

Like cloudiness, wind speed increases at higher altitudes instead of close to the equator at 0 degrees. The highest wind speeds above 20 mph were recorded between 60 and 80 degrees latitude. Most of the data points around 0 degrees latitude only showed wind speeds between 0-5 mph.
